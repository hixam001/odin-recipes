Lasagna

Home

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish, featuring layers of flat pasta, meat sauce (often with beef or sausage), ricotta or cottage cheese, and mozzarella, all baked together until bubbly and golden. It's a flavorful and satisfying casserole, often served as a main course.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lean pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onions
  • 1 can crushed tomatos
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/8 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound small-curd cottage cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 package shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Cook ground beef in a large deep skillet over medium heat and stir until browned and crumbly
  3. Add onion and cook until translucent
  4. Stir in crushed tomato sauce, 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, garlic, basil, salt, oregano, and sugar, Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally for 30 minutes
  5. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil. Cook lasagna noodles in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to bite. Drain and set aside. While noodles are cooking preheat the over to 375 degrees F
  6. Mix cottage cheese,parmesan cheese, eggs, remaining 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, salt, and pepper in large bowl until combined
  7. Assemble lasagna: Spread a spoon or two of sause over the bottom of a baking dish to coat it, place 2 layers of noodles over the sauce to cover
  8. Layer with 1/2 cheese mixture, 1/2 of the remaining sauce and 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers once more using the remaining noodles , cheese mixture and mozzarella. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes. Remove the foil and bake until cheese is golden brown, 5 to 10 more minutes.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it rest for 10 minutes before cutting and serving
